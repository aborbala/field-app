1. DATA_OVERVIEW

FIELD // COLLECTOR is a client-side geolocation utility. All GPS data capture and polygon drafting occur exclusively on your local device. We do not maintain any external database for user locations.

2. PERMISSIONS

The application requires access to your browser's Geolocation API. This is used solely to live-track your position for point and boundary collection. You can revoke this permission at any time via your browser settings.

3. STORAGE & COOKIES

FIELD // COLLECTOR does not use tracking cookies. We use Local Storage exclusively to persist your survey data on your active session. This data is non-persistent; it is deleted if you manually clear your browser cache or use the "PURGE" features within the Database sheet.

4. THIRD_PARTY_SYSTEMS

• LeafletJS & Tiles: Map tiles are streamed from OpenStreetMap/CARTO. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.
• Google AdSense: This site may use Google AdSense for monetization. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads. You can opt-out at Google's Ad Settings.

5. USER_RIGHTS (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise these rights by using the PURGE function in the database or clearing your browser cache.

6. CONTACT

For privacy concerns, contact: support.fieldcollector@example.com
